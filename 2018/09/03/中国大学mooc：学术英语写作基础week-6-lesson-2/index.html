中国大学MOOC：学术英语写作基础Week 6 Lesson 2
2018-09-03|MOOC笔记mooc大学英语学术写作

Task 1 Useful Expressions
1.蜿蜒狭窄的公路一次只能容两辆车通过。
The winding barrow road is accessible by only two cars at a time.
2.敢于挑战社会规范和习俗
Venture above and beyond society’s norms and conventions
3.探索未知的领域
Explore uncharted territories
4.无尽的喜悦和期待油然而生
Feel an immense amount of joy and anticipation swell up
5.天色将晚，渔船归港。
The day was at an end and the boats were headed home.
6.云朵变幻出一大片鲜亮的颜色。
The clouds were transformed into an array of vibrant colors.
7.晚风轻柔地吹在身上。
The night breeze blew gently over us.
8.她逃离了如蝉茧般束缚自己的社会规范，去寻找自我。
She escapes her closed, cocoon-like existence and is able to finally realize herself.
9.我们如此幸运能够见到这般神奇而又独特的时刻。
A magical and unique moment that we were lucky enough to witness.
10.海水既可以让你生，亦可以让你死。
The waters have creative and destructive potential.
11.就像Grand Isle 是天涯海角，Chopin和Edna也处在社会的边缘。
Just as Grand Isle is the furthest edge of land so too did Chopin and Edna take their position in society.
12.她把事情推向极致，挑战了不可挑战的极限。
She took things to the limit, to the edge and questioned the unquestionable.
