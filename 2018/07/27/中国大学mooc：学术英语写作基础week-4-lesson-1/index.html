Where do we put the topic sentence/thesis statement? 4. What makes a good topic sentence/thesis statement? What is a topic sentence or thesis statement? Topic Sentence/Thesis Statement is: Topic + Controlling Idea Topic: Limited subject Controlling Idea: The writer’s attitude toward or idea about the limited subject What does a topic sentence/thesis statement do? A topic sentence controls Topic Sentence -> control -> • Specific Support • Specific Support • Specific Support Specific Support -> Support -> Topic Sentence Introduction: Thesis Statement Body Paragraph 1: Topic Sentence & Specific Support Body Paragraph 2: Topic Sentence & Specific Support Body Paragraph 3: Topic Sentence & Specific Support Conclusion: Reworded thesis statements Where do we put the topic sentence/thesis statement? Topic Sentence -> At the beginning of a paragraph -> Make point clear and save readers’ time Thesis Statement -> At the end of the introduction -> Make point clear and save readers’ time What makes a good topic sentence/thesis statement? • Narrow down • Manageable Summary 1.Elements √A topic sentence/thesis statement = a topic + a controlling idea. 2.Control √A topic sentence controls a paragraph and a thesis statement controls the whole essay. 3.Position √Topic sentence at the beginning of each body paragraph. √Thesis statement at the end of the introduction. 4.Principle √A topic sentence and a thesis statement should be specific and manageable.
