    Lesson 2-Article Analysis ： is College Worth It

    Task 1 Useful Expressions

    以下表达为原文摘录，看不懂的去看原文结合上下文，附件是单元导读和文章及练习作业

    1. 债务很容易被偿还 This obligation would have been easy to discharge. 2. 找到一份收入丰厚的工作 Land a well-paid job 3. 做出勇敢的决定 Take the gutsy decision 4. 她的债务涨到了六万五千美元 Her debt has swollen to $65,000 5. 有些专业能收回成本，有些则不然 Some degress pay for themselves, others don’t. 6. 而事实更为微妙 The truth is more nuanced…… 7. 并不是所有的学位都同样有用 But not all degrees are equally useful. 8. 将上大学的费用计算在内 Factor in the cost of a degree 9. 20年后获得回报率为50万美元 Generate a 20-year return of $500,000 10. 艺术和人文各专业区别很大。毫无疑问，他们虽然都能滋养灵魂，但并不都能填鼓钱包。 Arts and humanities courses are much more varied. All doubtless nourish the soul, but not all fatten the wallet. 11. 获得丰厚的回报 Pay off handsomely 12. 购买二十年期国库券 Plonk the money in 20-year treasury bills 13. 41%国内顶尖大学毕业生无法在自己选择的领域找到工作 Some 41% of graduates from the nation’s top colleges could not find jobs in their chosen field. 14. 做出明智的选择 Make more informed choices 15. 那些无法做到物有所值的学校，要么好自为之，要么彻底消失。 Those that offer poor value for money will have to shape up, or disappear.
