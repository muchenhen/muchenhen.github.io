    Foundation of academic English writing, College of foreign languages, Xiamen university,China university moocs Week3：Paragraph Construction Lesson 1-Writing Skill ：Support

    Contents What are supporting details? How to write a well-supported paragraph? 1.What are supporting details?

    • Explains
    • Defines
    • Proves an idea

    Supporting details include

    • Examples
    • Statistics
    • Statements by anthorities
    • Reasons
    • Details
    • Results
    • Procedures
    • Classification
    • Solutions
    • Analogies
    • Comparison

    Examples

    • Quick examples

    A list of things \ instances \ occurrences that exemplify the topic

    • Narrative example

    Story or description of a single occurrence which illustrates the point 2.Statements by Authorities An authorities is “a person who is in a position to know about a subject.” Document our source

    When not using our own

    • Observations
    • Experimental results
    • Experiences

    Summary 1、A supporting detail explains, defines or proves an idea. 2、Ways to support a topic sentence: examples, statistics, and statements by authorities. 3、An authority is a person in the position to know about a subject.
