What is unity?
One main point
How to achieve unity?
A checklist

Checklist
1.An essay has one main idea and this main idea is expressed in a thesis statement.
2.A paragraph has one main idea and this main idea is expressed in a topic sentence.
3.The topic sentence of each body paragraph supports the thesis statement of the essay.
4.Each sentence in a paragraph supports the topic sentence.
5.The conclusion restates the thesis statement at the end of the essay.

Task 1 Useful Expressions

1.人们对登月反响的电视画面
Footage of the world’s reaction to the lunar landing
2.这一点从一开始就很清楚了。
The clues had been there from the start.
3.他们真正感叹的不是身下死灰色的星球，而是亮蓝色的地球。
Their real wonder was not at the dead gray planet beneath them, but at the vibrant blue globe in the distance.
4.带着更深的敬意
With a much deeper sense of reverence
5.好奇心永远得不到满足，它总是在寻求着新的目标。
Curiosity is never satisfied, it merely finds new targets.
6.最有天赋的宇航员
The most naturally gifted astronaut
7.成就本身并不重要，重要的追求的过程。
Achievements themselves aren’t what count, it’s the fact that you woeked for them.
8.感叹人类生活的多样性。
Marvel at the variety of human life.
9.往往到未知世界的探索却会人们更多地了解旅行者的家乡。
As so often, a journey into the unknown had revealed more about the traveler’s home than about the destination.
10.更好地了解了地球的脆弱
With a deep sense of the Earth’s fragility
11.你的观念发生了变化。
A shift takes place in your perception.
12.你的想法就会开始不同。
And you start to think quite differently.
13.这一发现极大地促进了地球上刚刚兴起的环保运动地发展。
The discovery gave a big boost to the nascent Green movement.
14.立足本地，放眼全球。
Think globally, act locally.
15.阿波罗倒计时结束升空的那一瞬间，就是全球化起步的那一刻。
T minus zero for Apollo was T plus one for globalization.
16.渴望新经历
Yearn for new experiences
17.我们对给我们泼冷水的人感到不满。
We resent anyone who dampens our excitement.
18.我向……表达了他的看法
He voiced his thoughts to
19.我意识到人类的本性就是不断地探索。
I realize there’s a fundamental truth to our nature: man must explore.
20.生活是个永恒的循环。
Life as a perpetual cycle.
21.概括了阿波罗计划的本质。
Sums up the very essence of the Apollo missions.
22.旅行不是你去过哪里，而是你发现自己。
Travel isn’t about where you’re going, it’s about who you are.
23.我们不能停止探索的脚步。
We shall not cease from exploration.

Conciseness and Emphasis What is conciseness? “Concise writing mankes every woed count”(Aeron 129). NO useless words What is emphasis? “Emphatic writing leads your readers to see your main ideas both within and among sentences”(Aeron 99). NOT leave your reader guessing and wondering about the point How to achieve conciseness and emphasis? 1.Focus on the subject and verb Sentence A: The reason why most of the country shift to daylight savings time is that winter days are much shorter than summer days. Sentence B: Most of the country shift to daylight savings time because winter days are much shorter than summer days.(concise) 2.Avoid using nouns derived from verbs Sentence A: The intention of the company was to expand its workforce. A proposal was also made to diversify the backgrounds and abilities of their employees. PS: Obscures the key action of the sentence Sentence B: The company intended to expand its workforce. It also proposed to diversity the backgrounds and abilities of their employees. 3.Avoid using weak verbs “be” “make” “have” Sentence A: Ashard as this is to believe, China is now the leader of globalization. Sentence B: As hard as this is to believe, China is now leading globalization. 4.Avoid using passive voice Sentence A: It is believed by the candidate that a ceiling must be palced on the budget by Congress. Sentence B: The candidate believes that Congress must place a ceiling on the budget. 5.Avoid using empty words Examples: All things considered In a manner of speaking For all intents and purposes For the most part In my opinion Last but not least More or less As far as I am concerned As we all know that It cannot be denied that 6.Avoid using wordy expressions At all times Always At the persent time Now,yet Because of the fact that Because By virtue of the fact that Because Due to the fact that Because For the purpose of Because In the event that If In order to To In the final analysis finally 7.Begin with old information and end with new Not emphatic: a. Education almost means controversy these days, with rising costs and constant complaints about its inadequacies. b. But the value of schooling should not be obscured by the controversy. c. The single best means of economic advancement, despite its short-comings, remains education.

PAT乙级真题1001：A+B和C (15)

时间限制 1000 ms 内存限制 32768 KB 代码长度限制 100 KB 判断程序 Standard (来自 小小)

题目描述

给定区间[-2的31次方, 2的31次方]内的3个整数A、B和C，请判断A+B是否大于C。

输入描述**:**

输入第1行给出正整数T(<=10)，是测试用例的个数。随后给出T组测试用例，每组占一行，顺序给出A、B和C。整数间以空格分隔。

输出描述**:**

对每组测试用例，在一行中输出”Case #X: true”如果A+B>C，否则输出”Case #X: false”，其中X是测试用例的编号（从1开始）。

输入例子**:**

4

1 2 3

2 3 4

2147483647 0 2147483646

0 -2147483648 -2147483647

输出例子**:**

Case #1: false

Case #2: true

Case #3: true

Case #4: false
