Lesson 2-Article Analysis ： Women Earning More

Task 1 Useful Expressions

以下表达为原文摘录，看不懂的去看原文结合上下文，附件是单元导读和文章及练习作业

1、限制妇女职业生涯的玻璃天花板 The glass ceilings constraining women’s careers 2、与之相应的自然是…… It follows almost naturally that…… 3、夫妇相对收入模式令人吃惊 The pattern of relative income of men and women at young(ish) ages in a marriage is striking. 4、这一模式并不是受老年夫妇的收入模式的影响而得出的 And this pattern is not driven by older couples. 5、……的社会观念依然盛行 The social norm " …… " seems to be alive and well. 6、这不仅仅是个奇怪的现象而已，这一事实有着深远的后果 That is not only a curious fact; it has consequences. 7、有潜力做某事的女性 Women with the potential to earn more than their husbands 8、类似家庭里面的同类女性 Simial women in comparable families 9、他们没有完全发挥他们的赚钱潜力 They use their earnings potential to a lower degree. 10、对经济造成了负面影响。 That’s bad news for the economy. 11、论文提出了有关……的一些线索 The paper offers some hints as to…… 12、习俗影响了家务分工 Norms affect the division of household chores. 13、有权利期望丈夫多承担家务 She might rightful expect him to take on some additional responsibilities at home. 14、安慰某人因……而感到的不安 Feel compelled to soothe their husbands’ unease at earning less 15、婚姻危机加剧，离婚率上升 Marriage difficulties jump and divorce rates increase. 16、只多一点点还是多很多 Only slightly more or divorce rates increase. 17、社会习俗不易打破 Norms are stubborn things. 18、针对性的政策 Targeted policies Steps for Writing a Summary 1.
