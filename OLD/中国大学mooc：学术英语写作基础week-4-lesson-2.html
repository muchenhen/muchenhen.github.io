Women in Leadship 1、我和大家意起祝贺你们通过不懈努力最终拿到学位。 I join so many others in celebrating and applauding the hard work and perseverance that earned you your degree. 2、成绩不能完全归功于自己。 No one accomplishes anything all alone. 3、一路上培养呵护你们 nurture you along the way 4、指明未来的方向 pinpoint a path to the future 5、我写此文呼吁你们承担起提高每个人福祉的伟大事业。 I am writing to appeal to you to talk on a important cause that will improve the lives of all. 6、经济学规律和其他学科的研究结果都表明 The laws of economics and studies of diversity tell us … 7、充分挖掘人力资源和他们的智慧 tap the entire pool of human resources and talent 8、对家庭生活的研究表明 Studies of home life tell us … 9、深入考虑两性平等的问题 give much thought to gender inequality 10、抓住机会 seize those opportunities 11、但是20年过去了，妇女仍然与平等相距甚远。 But more than 20 years later, we are nowhere near equality for women. 12、在公司化的美国，女性高管的数量在过去十年几乎没有增加。 Women in executive roles in corporate America has barely budged over the past decade. 13、平衡未来的事业和家庭 balance future careers and families 14、打破阻止女性进步的内部和外部的障碍 break down both the external and internal barriers that hold women back 15、把理想变为真正的平等 turn the promise of equality into true equality Why Men Fail 1、男生的学习成绩不如女生。 Male academic performance is lagging. 2、男生只拿到了40%的学士和硕士学位。 Only 40 percent of bachelor’s degrees go to men, along with 40 percent of master’s degrees. 3、男人的失业率也在上升。 Men are dropping out of the labor force. 4、男人依然占据着企业里面最顶尖的工作岗位。 Men still dominate the tippy-top of the corporate ladder. 5、接受了为什么男人经济地位下降的某个说法。 enbrace a certain theory to explain men’s economic decline. 6、在执行这些任务时表现很差 stink at these tasks 7、提出一个不同的理论 posit a different theory 8、男性比较死板，女性则更灵活 Men are more likely to be rigid; women are more fluid. 9、当社会发生巨变时，处于社会上层的人肯定会死守着旧秩序不放。 When there’s big social change, the people who were on the top of the old order are bound to cling to the old ways. 10、处于下层的人则一定会爆发出活力。 The people who were on the bottom are bound to experoence a burst of energy. 11、他们对新机会莫名地不感兴趣。 They are strangely immune to new options. 12、她们会接受能够给她们更多自由专心于事业的社会和性别规则。 They embrace social and sexual rules that give them the freedom to focus on their professional lives. 13、有很多证据支持她的观点。 There is a lot of evidence to support her case. 14、能够较好地调整离婚后的生活。 be better able to adjust to divorce. 15、男人则固守男性原则，从而极大限制了他们的视野和形动。 Men still adhere to the masculinity rules, which limits their vision and their movement. 附件： 第四单元 Women in leadership & why men fail导读 Article for Lecture 4 Exercises for Article of Lecture 4
